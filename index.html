Projects

Convenient Investing

Convenient Investing is a Python project implementing the tkinter library. For this project, my partner and I mapped the project architecture, developed the pseudocode, made a schedule, and coded. This project connected people with the stock market, worldwide news, expert teachers to learn, and bank accounts. Beyond the MVP for this project, we would implement realtime access to the stock market, more accurate tracking of owned shares, specific learning modules to teach beginners, and a chat room to share opinions and suggestions. This project was completed for the KSU Fall 2022 Hackathon by Assurant, "Helping People Thrive in a Connected World".

Find Your Paradise

Find Your Paradise is an ECL (Enterprise Control Language) project that helps people find places to live that maximize their quality of life and happiness. My partners and I were provided with a series of datasets and our task was to provide choices in an easy-to-use form to the user that when submitted will generate a unique set of scores based on locations (Ex: by State). This project was completed for the KSU Spring 2023 Hackathon by HPCC Systems, "For Social Good".

St. Theresa Reflection Area

The Eagle Scout Service Project gives a scout the opportunity to demonstrate leadership while performing a project for the benefit of their community. In my project I decided to build a reflection area at my local church, St. Theresa's Catholic Church. In the project, I built two benches, two flower beds, a library, as well as some landscaping. Between planning, organizing, and execution, this project took a grand total of 186 hours.
